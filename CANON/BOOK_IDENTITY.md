# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 041
**Title:** Bottom's Up Forever
**Slug:** alcohol-poisoning
**Category:** Self-Inflicted

## The Death (One Sentence)
A competitive American frat bro drinks himself to death during an escalating international drinking game competition, where cultural machismo and peer pressure override every bodily warning sign.

## Tagline
"He came to win. He left in a body bag."

## Themes
1. Toxic masculinity and the pressure to "prove" manhood through dangerous behavior
2. The dark side of hostel party culture and how it enables self-destruction
3. The gap between performative confidence and genuine self-awareness

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 45%
- Horror: 30%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's witnessed or participated in hostel drinking culture gone too far. Travelers who've seen someone pushed past their limits by group dynamics. Former party-goers who cringe at their younger selves.

## Comparison Titles
- *Animal House* meets *Midsommar*

## Content Warnings
- Graphic depiction of alcohol poisoning symptoms
- Vomiting/bodily fluids
- Peer pressure and coercion
- Death by substance abuse
